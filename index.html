Help & Quick Guide

New to PUPass? Follow these simple steps to book and manage your appointment.

How to Use PUPass

Here is a quick guide for first-time users:

1. Book an Appointment

Go to Services or tap Book Appointment, then choose the office you need such as Registrar, Cashier, Library, Guidance, Student Affairs, or Scholarship.

2. Choose Your Request, Date, and Time

Select your request type, pick an available appointment date, choose a time slot, and write your purpose of visit.

3. Review and Confirm

Check your student details, office, request type, date, time, and purpose before confirming your booking.

4. Save Your Queue Pass

After confirming, screenshot, print, or save your digital queue pass. Present it when you visit the office.

5. Track Your Queue

Use Queue Status to check your position, students ahead, and whether your number is already being served.

Need Technical Support?

If something is not working, you may open a technical ticket below.

Report login errors, booking errors, queue status problems, QR code problems, calendar problems, or buttons that are not working.

Do not use this for regular office concerns. For appointment status, use My Appointment or Queue Status.
